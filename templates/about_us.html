About CatelogIQ

Empowering businesses with AI-driven data solutions for smarter insights and decision-making.

Who We Are

CatelogIQ is a pioneering technology company dedicated to transforming how businesses interact with their data. Founded by a team of passionate data scientists and engineers, we specialize in AI-driven solutions for log analytics and text categorization.

Our mission is to empower organizations to unlock the full potential of their data through intuitive, scalable, and intelligent tools. Whether it’s detecting anomalies in real-time logs or categorizing vast amounts of unstructured text, CatelogIQ delivers actionable insights that drive success.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every organization, regardless of size, can harness the power of AI to make informed decisions. By combining cutting-edge machine learning with user-friendly interfaces, we aim to democratize data analytics and make it accessible to all.
